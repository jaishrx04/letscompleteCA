Overview

The Chartered Accountancy (CA) course is a professional course in the field of accounting and finance. It is globally recognized and offers a range of opportunities in various industries. The course is offered by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

Eligibility

  • 10+2 or equivalent education from a recognized board.
  • Age limit between 18 to 30 years.
  • Must pass Common Proficiency Test (CPT).

Syllabus

The CA course consists of 4 levels:

  1. CPT
  2. Integrated Professional Competence Course (IPCC)
  3. 3-year articleship training
  4. Final exam

Exams

The CA exams are held twice a year, in May and November. The exams consist of multiple choice questions, objective type questions, and descriptive type questions.

Career Opportunities

Chartered Accountants can find opportunities in fields such as audit and assurance, taxation, finance, consulting, and more. They can work in government organizations, private companies, or start their own practices.
